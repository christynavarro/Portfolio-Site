On The Rocks

On the Rocks is a recipe-based app housing a variety of cocktail recipes. Users can sign up for a subscription service
to receive a monthly package with ingredients to create each recipe from home. The user will have access to a database
of several cocktail recipes where they can follow along to make their favorite cocktails at home or try something new.

Research

The first step of the design process was to begin identifying a target audience. Several user interviews were conducted
to get an understanding of what each user was looking for in a subscription-based recipe app. The research showed that users
valued easy to read recipes and placed high value on appealing imagery. Users wanted to use a platform that curated fun recipes
aligned with their personalized preferences.

My Process

After determining the target demographic, the next stage in the design process was outlining the user flows. This helped
to identify different entry points and actions the users would take while navigating through the platform.
These tasks included the following:
1. Searching for different recipes.
2. Viewing the instructions for creating the recipe
3. Viewing what is in this month’s subscription

Wireframing

After outlining the user flow, I was able to design the wire frames, beginning with the low fidelity wire frames.
During the low fidelity wireframing process, I drew which UI elements would allow users to complete an action.
Once that was done, I was able to lay them out on Adobe XD to get a better understanding of the interface.

Ideation

Once the wireframes had been developed, a mood board was developed to establish the visual direction of the app.
Photos were incorporated to represent the type of imagery and mood that the app aimed to evoke from the users.
A preliminary color palette was developed, as well. The imagery used to inspire the high-fidelity wireframes use
bright colors and have a fun, refreshing feeling.

Design

On the Rocks was created for 3 different screen sizes. Each screen has been designed being mindful of different
break points to ensure a seamless user experience regardless of which device it is being used on.

Mock Ups

After finishing the High Fidelity wireframe design, I created mock ups to show the final product in its intended use

Case Study

Conclusion

Many people get frustrated using recipe-based apps due to lengthy introductions, pictures that don’t give them an
accurate idea of what they’re making or find it difficult to follow the instructions. On the Rocks has solved these
issues by creating recipes that are straight forward and easy to follow along. The imagery used through out is clear
and instructions are laid out in a way that’s easy to read. Ingredient lists include pictures so the user knows what
to look for if they choose to make a recipe on their own. The layout is easy to navigate through to avoid users getting
overwhelmed with information.
